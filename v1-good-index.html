A Few Tips

  • Say “I feel.....when you.....because.....” and when someone says that to you, reply with “What I heard you say is...”
  • What you/I feel is real.
  • Stop arguing and ask “How you are feeling?”.
  • Don't blame.
  • Admit to being wrong.
