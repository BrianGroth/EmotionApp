About EmotionApp

EmotionApp is a prototype mobile app for noticing, naming, and understanding emotions in everyday life.

About the prototype

This early version uses five emotional ranges to help people find language for what they are feeling, consider where they might be on a spectrum, and explore a few practical next steps.

Who made it

EmotionApp was developed by Brian Groth, with design input from Lorie Groth.

A work in progress

This is a prototype, so the content and interface will continue to evolve as the ideas are tested and refined.
