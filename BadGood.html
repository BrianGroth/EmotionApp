About This Emotion Pair

Emotions at the Bad end of this range include Loathing, Revolted, and Awful to Timid, Pessimistic, and Hope in the middle, to Releaf, Satisfaction, and Grateful at the Good end of the range.

This range of emotions:

Loathing, Detestable, Abhorrent, Repugnant, Revolted, Revulsion, Disgust, Disgusted, Contempt, Judgmental, Awful, Disapproval, Disappointed, Disillusioned, Aversion, Avoidant, Hesitant, Hesitation, Timid, Doubt, Pessimism, Neutral, Tolerance, Open-minded, Acceptance, Hope, Encouraging, Optimism, Positivity, Relief, Satisfaction, Appreciation, Goodness, Gratitude, Fulfilled, Inspired, Uplifted.
